## ■ 文法ミス：7個 / 4.7%

### 修正対象と方法

1. **第1段落3文目**

   * 【誤文】First, having more vacation time help workers keep their family’s relationship good.
   * **理由**：主語（having more vacation time）が単数扱いのため動詞は “helps” が正しい
   * **修正**：First, having more vacation time **helps** workers keep their family’s relationship good.

2. **第1段落4文目**

   * 【誤文】…they can spend a lot with their family…
   * **理由**：“spend a lot” の後に “of time” が抜けている
   * **修正**：…they can spend a lot **of time** with their family…

3. **第1段落4文目**

   * 【誤文】…take their families to far places for travel.
   * **理由**：不自然な前置詞・語順
   * **修正**：…take their families to **distant places** for travel.

4. **第1段落5文目**

   * 【誤文】Therefore families feel amuse and workers can maintain their relationship.
   * **理由**：“amuse” は動詞、“amused” が正しい形容詞
   * **修正**：Therefore families feel **amused**, and workers can maintain their relationship.

5. **第1段落6文目**

   * 【誤文】Second, vacation will play another important role in economy.
   * **理由**：”economy” の前に冠詞 “the” が必要
   * **修正**：Second, vacation will play another important role in **the** economy.

6. **第1段落7文目**

   * 【誤文】For instance, there are general thinking that more and more working can earn a lot of money…
   * **理由**：”general thinking” は不適切、“working” と “more and more” の語順も不自然
   * **修正**：For instance, **there is a general belief that working more and more** can earn a lot of money…

7. **第1段落8文目**

   * 【誤文】By taking vacation can relax worker’s body and mental, so workers can earn a lot of money efficiently and they can help their family’s life in cost.
   * **理由**：主語が抜け、“vacation” の冠詞欠落、“worker’s body and mental” の表現も不適切
   * **修正**：**By taking a vacation, workers can relax their bodies and minds**, so they can earn money more efficiently and help support their families financially.
