## ■ 文法ミス：2個 / 1.34%

### 修正対象と方法

* **【誤文】**（第1段落2文目）

  > entertainment which movies and TV programs have influences young people to think more creatively, thus they come to tell more exciting things and they will be able to give good feelings such as excitement and happiness to people around them.

  * **理由**：関係代名詞節と主語・動詞の構造が不適切
  * **修正**：the entertainment in movies and TV programs influences young people to think more creatively; thus, they start sharing more exciting ideas and can bring positive feelings—like excitement and happiness—to those around them.
  * **修正箇所**：第2段落1文目

* **【誤文】**（第3段落3文目）

  > knowing own culture lets people to do good behavior.

  * **理由**：「let」の後の不定詞に不要な “to” が入っている
  * **修正**：knowing their own culture lets people behave well.
  * **修正箇所**：第3段落3文目

* **第1段落2文目** “watching the contents of movies and TV programs makes young people behave funny.”

* **第2段落1文目** 長過ぎる文を分割し、表現を簡潔化
* **第3段落1文目** “they will be kind by watching their own culture’s introduction.”

  * **言い換え** → they become kinder by watching programs that introduce their own culture.
* **第4段落1文目** “more positive influence than negative on how young people behave.”

  * **句読点追加・語順調整** → have a more positive than negative influence on young people’s behavior.
