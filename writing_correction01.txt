## ■ 文法ミス：5個 / 3.33%

### 修正対象と方法（修正箇所の文を全文明示し、段落・文番号も明示）

* **【誤文】**（第1段落2文目）
  "First, having more vacation time help workers keep their family’s relationship good."

  * **理由**：主語（having more vacation time）が単数扱いなので動詞はhelpではなくhelpsが正しい
  * **修正**："First, having more vacation time helps workers keep their family’s relationship good."
  * **修正箇所**：第1段落2文目

* **【誤文】**（第2段落2文目）
  "they can spend a lot with their family and do many things such as play with their kids and take their families to far places for travel."

  * **理由**："spend a lot with their family"は不自然で "spend a lot of time with their family"が正しい。"take their families" は "family" と単数扱いに統一する方が自然。"far places for travel" は "distant places"や "places far away for travel"などがより自然
  * **修正**："they can spend a lot of time with their family and do many things such as play with their kids and take their family to distant places for travel."
  * **修正箇所**：第2段落2文目

* **【誤文】**（第2段落3文目）
  "Therefore families feel amuse and workers can maintain their relationship."

  * **理由**："feel amuse"は文法的に誤り。"amuse"は動詞。ここは形容詞 "amused" が正しい。"maintain their relationship" は "maintain good relationships" など複数形の方が自然
  * **修正**："Therefore families feel amused and workers can maintain good relationships."
  * **修正箇所**：第2段落3文目

* **【誤文】**（第3段落1文目）
  "vacation will play another important role in economy."

  * **理由**："vacation"は不可算名詞で単数扱いだが、ここは一般的概念として複数形 "vacations" や "taking vacations" のほうが自然。"in economy" は "in the economy" が正しい
  * **修正**："Vacations will play another important role in the economy."
  * **修正箇所**：第3段落1文目

* **【誤文】**（第3段落3文目）
  "By taking vacation can relax worker’s body and mental, so workers can earn a lot of money efficiently and they can help their family’s life in cost."

  * **理由**："By taking vacation can relax..."は主語がない不完全文。"taking vacations can relax workers’ bodies and minds"が正しい。 "worker’s body and mental"も単数で不自然。"family’s life in cost" は意味不明。"support their family financially"などが適切
  * **修正**："By taking vacations, workers can relax their bodies and minds, so they can earn money more efficiently and support their family financially."
  * **修正箇所**：第3段落3文目
